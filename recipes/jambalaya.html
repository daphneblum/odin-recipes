Jambalaya

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 lb smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp Cajun seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions, bell peppers, celery, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the sliced sausage to the pot and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add the rice and stir to coat it with the oil and vegetables.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and stir in the Cajun seasoning. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Add the shrimp to the pot and cook until they turn pink, about 5 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot.

Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 1 lb smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp Cajun seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the flour and stir continuously to make a roux. Cook until it turns a dark brown color.
  2. Add the chopped onions, bell peppers, celery, and garlic to the roux. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the diced chicken and sliced sausage to the pot. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and stir in the Cajun seasoning. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot over cooked rice.

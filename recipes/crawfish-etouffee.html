Crawfish Etouffee

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb crawfish tails
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups seafood stock
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp Cajun seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and stir continuously to make a roux. Cook until it turns a light brown color.
  2. Add the chopped onions, bell peppers, celery, and garlic to the roux. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the crawfish tails to the pot and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Pour in the seafood stock and stir in the Cajun seasoning. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot over cooked rice.
